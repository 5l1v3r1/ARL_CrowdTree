Coordinating conjunctions

Coordinating conjunctions are words like and, or, nor, and but. Trees with these words may look a little messy, but that's ok. The general rule is that the first word/phrase/clause should be put on top.

John and Mary live in a cottage.

The words yet, so, and for are also sometimes used as coordinating conjunctions. The words either, neither, and both sometimes occur with coordinating conjunctions.
Either it will rain or it will snow.
