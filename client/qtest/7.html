Apposition

Apposition involves putting two things side-by-side. It is common to surround the second part using punctuation such as commas, parentheses, or dashes. In the examples below, because the second part is modifying/rephrasing/clarifying the first part, the second part is put underneath.
John, the brother of Joe, looked on quietly.
The cost of postage is only 1500 yen (about $14).
