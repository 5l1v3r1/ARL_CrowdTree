Introduction

Welcome! The goal of this task is to correct a grammar tree for the given sentence. We're not expecting you to be a grammar expert to work on this, but maybe you'll be one by the end! (Actually, if you are trained in linguistics, you may find some of our rules a bit odd.)

Drag and drop the circles to adjust the tree structure. You can zoom in and out using your mouse scroll wheel.

Each word is represented by a yellow circle. To change the tree structure, click on a circle and then drag it to its new attachment site. When you drag the circle, drop zones will appear, showing where it can be connected. Once released over one of these zones, the word will move into its new location.

Try adjusting the tree on the right. To change the position of the entire tree on the page, you can click in background (the white area) and drag the mouse.

The tree should look like this when complete.

To pass this qualification test, you must edit each tree in the space to the right. When you complete a tree, click Next in the lower navigation bar to continue onto the next one. At the end, you'll get to submit the test.
