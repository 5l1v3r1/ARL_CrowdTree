The word "that"

The word that has a few different uses. In the sentence below, it is used in a clause acting as a verb object (that they were terrified).

I saw that they were terrified.
In the sentence below, that is used in a clause acting as a noun complement (that he bought at the store).
He ate the lollipop that he bought at the store.

Sometimes, the word that will occur in the sort of position where a word like the or a could be.

Don't buy that cake!

Another example

Don't buy that!
