Qualification Test for Grammar Tree Creation/Editing

Welcome!

Completing this test with a satisfactory score grants you the qualification necessary to work on some of our tree annotation HITs. To pass this qualification HIT, you must edit each tree in the space to the right according to the guidelines given.

We are not expecting you to be a grammar expert. Hopefully, you'll gain confidence as you go.

Feel free to include any comments you have in the comment box.

There are 12 trees to work through. After you are done with all of them, you'll be able to submit your test. Click Accept HIT to continue.
